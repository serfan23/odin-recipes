Salad Shirazi

Description

Another classic, popular Persian recipe, this time a side dish! Considered Iran's 'national salad' and a definite favorite for the summertime, Salad Shirazi is often paired with dry rice dishes or even just a layer of Lavash.

This recipe is relatively quick and easy, and can act as a delicious side dish to pair with rice, or even just enjoyed by itself!

Here's how you can make this salad at home!

Ingredients

    2 tomatoes diced 1 cucumber diced 1/4 - 1/2 red onion diced 1-2 tablespoons lime juice or 1 lime 1 tablespoon olive oil Salt and pepper to your liking Chopped mint for garnish

    Steps

      Stir all ingredients together and serve in a bowl. Enjoy!
